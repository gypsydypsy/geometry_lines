
3D formats : 

        - OBJ, FBX, PLY, COLLADA, 3DS, GLTF...
        - GLTF is becoming the standard and covers most of our needs
        - GLTF supports : geometries, materials, cameras, lights, scene graph, animations, skeletons, morphing...


GLTF Formats : 

        - GLTF : 
            > Multiple files : .gltf (can be opened like json), .bin (binary), .png (textures), editable
        
        - GLTF binary : 
            > Single files : .bin, contains all the data but not editable, usually lighter and fastest to load

        - Draco GLTF : 
            > Draco is a compression format, lighter than simple GLTF

        - GLTF embed : 
            > Single json file, but textures and buffer data are embedded in it, the heaviest

   
Which one to choose ? 
        > Depends on what you want to do
            > Editable or not ? 
            > Lighter or heavier ?
